# App Branding & Name Ideas

AI-powered home furnishing app — users visualize and customize their space with real shop products: furniture, rugs, TVs, kitchen layouts, wall finishes, and more. Pick models, apply colors, and preview the result before buying.

This document lists **100 brandable names** that are:

- **Persian-ish** (romanized Farsi roots) **or** clean **English**
- **AI-related** — intelligence, vision, render, generate, smart design
- **No `ck` or `dw`** in the handle (e.g. no *craft*, *dwell*, *mock*, *sketch*)

Instagram handles were **verified available** on **29 June 2026**.

> **Disclaimer:** Availability changes quickly. Re-check `instagram.com/<handle>` before registering.
